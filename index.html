Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Propelion?

A: Propelion is a fun, gamified way of learning key sales skills useful for your job. You complete modules, write reflections, and compete against your fellow teammates.

Q: What happens if I miss a module?

A: We make sure to give you a generous time window to complete modules. If you miss a module for any reason, you won't receive any points for that module, and will not be eligible for the final certificate. Even if you missed a module, though, it is still worth it to try to get points in the other ones, so that you can finish in the best position you can in your team!
